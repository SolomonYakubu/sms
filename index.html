Roadmap

Genesis Meme Drop

Engage the community with an initial meme drop featuring the likenesses of crypto influencers, developers, and regulatory figures in a humorous setting reminiscent of the last supper.

Meme Mining

Introduce a meme mining mechanism where users can contribute and vote for memes to earn $SMS rewards, fostering community participation.

Launch a meme creation contest with significant $SMS rewards for the most creative and shareable memes related to the crypto community and its characters

NFT Marketplace

Establish an NFT marketplace for unique and limited edition meme-inspired NFTs, allowing users to trade and showcase their favorite crypto memes as collectibles.

Decentralized Governance

Implement a decentralized governance system where $SMS holders can propose and vote on future meme drops, collaborations, and community initiatives.
